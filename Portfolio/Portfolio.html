Who am I ?

Hello, my name is Conor Brooke, and I'm an aspiring Software Developer.
Over the last month I have been working heavily on my skills in Web
Development. This Portfolio is a showcase of all my work during that
period, and I'm delighted to finally be sharing it. I have a passion for
computers, and especially for the software that makes them functional
to a user. I am fascinated with the concepts behind some of most popular
applications today, and I'm hopeful that someday I can understand and
build similar projects.


Outside of my work with computers, I am interested in video games,
fitness and philosophy, particularly Greek philosophy and ethics. I also
enjoy spending time with friends and family. I am dedicated and passionate
about anything I undertake. My dream is to turn my love for software into
a sustainable income somewhere in the near future. I hope you find some
interest in my work so far.

Future Aims

My hopes for the journey ahead is to complete all courses for both
Front-end, and Back-end on FreeCodeCamps. My first step on this
journey was the completion of the Responsive Web-Design course.
Finally finishing this step has given me a lot of motivation to keep
moving forward on my journey, and I have had lots of fun making
these simple projects. My next challenge will be facing JavaScript,
this will be the first programming language that I tackle which I'm
sure shall bring many challenges in and of itself. I'm, however, looking
forward to learning and mastering this language as I have already
discovered areas for its use in my projects.


I haven't really though much of what I will do after JavaScript, but I
do hope to continue learning and maybe even begin my decent into
the Back-end of Programming. However, before then there are other
skills I want to improve and master such as SASS, React, Adobe XD,
Photoshop, and basic Terminal knowledge.
